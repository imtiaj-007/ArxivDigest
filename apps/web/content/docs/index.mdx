ArxivDigest is an autonomous daily AI agent that scans new arxiv `cs.AI`, `cs.LG`,
and `cs.CL` submissions, produces structured TL;DRs, classifies them by theme, and
ranks them by novelty and impact — all on free-tier infrastructure, $0/month.

## Reading the digest

Each paper card shows:

- **Theme badges** — 1–3 taxonomy slugs, most relevant first (see [taxonomy](/docs/themes)).
- **Impact score** — blended novelty (cosine distance to neighbors in embedding
  space) + LLM impact judgment, on a 0–1 scale. Most papers land in `0.4–0.6`;
  see [methodology → rank](/docs/methodology#rank).
- **Structured TL;DR** — Problem / Approach / Result / Why it matters. Grounded
  strictly in the abstract; the prompt forbids inventing claims.
